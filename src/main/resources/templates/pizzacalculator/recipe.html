The recipe

  • 4 pizzas

The ingredients

The dough
  • 700 g tipo 0 flour
  • 500 g water
  • 10 g yeast
  • 10 g yeast
  • 20 g salt
The Tomato Sauce
  • 1 can of tomatoes
  • A handful of basil leafes
  • A pinch of salt
The dough
  • 400 g mozarella cheese
  • Fresh basil
  • Virgin Olive Oil

The instructions

Step 1: The Poolish
Mix together 200 g water, 5 g honey, and 10 g fresh yeast. Then add and mix in 200 g Tipo 0 flour. Leave in fridge for 16-24 hours with a cover.
Step 2: Mix the dough

Take out the poolish. Leave it 1 hour at room temperature. Then add 500 g Tipo 0 flour. Mix in 300 water and 20 g of salt. A little at a time.

Do some slap and folds. Let rest for 15 minutes. Put a little oil on your hands and fold the dough into one large ball. Add a little oil on top, tap, tap. Cover with a dish towel. Rest for one hour.

Step 3: Finish the dough
Make 3 balls. Put a little oil on your hands. Fold the dough into itself. Close it in. Leave the balls on a sheet of baking paper, 4 fingers appart. Add a little oil on top, tap, tap. Cover with plastic foil and let it rest and raise for 1-2 hours.
Step 4: Sauce and filling
Pour the tomatoes into a bowl and mash them with your hands. Add salt. Cut the mozarella into straight chuncks. Not too small, not too large. Prepare some basil leaves to put on top.
Step 5: Compose
Roll each dough ball in flour, place it on a floured surface. Press it down, pinch it in the middle and outwards to press air to the circumference. Press, pull, rotate until you have a nice round shape. Add tomato sauce, cheese chunks, a little basil, drip some virgin olive oil on top. In particular on the basil leaves. Slide in oven and bake for 5-6 minutes at max temperature. Preferably on a steel og stone plate.
Step 6: Enjoy
When the pizza is done: take it out, add a little parmesan on top, a little extra basil, and a little extra virgin oil. Slice it. Serve it. Enjoy it. Bon apetite.
